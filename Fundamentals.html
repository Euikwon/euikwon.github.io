In volleyball, there are 6 basic fundamentals that are essential. They are:

Offence

  • Spiking- an action to hit the ball over the net to make it hard for the opposition to return.
  • Serving- an action to put the ball in play and start the rally
  • Setting- an action to get the ball over to the spiker to hit

Defence

  • Passing- an action to get the ball to the setter
  • Blocking- an action to stop spikes getting over the net
  • Digging- an action to prevent the ball from hitting the ground
